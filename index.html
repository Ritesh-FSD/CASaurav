Our Practice Areas

Comprehensive Services for Every Financial Need

From compliance to consultancy, we offer an integrated suite of chartered accountancy services tailored for businesses, startups, trusts, and individuals.

Statutory Audit

Comprehensive audit of financial statements as mandated under the Companies Act, ensuring true and fair reporting.

Tax Audit

Audit under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act for businesses and professionals exceeding prescribed turnover limits.

Bank Concurrent Audit

Real-time examination of bank branch transactions to detect irregularities, fraud, and compliance gaps.

Internal Audit

Independent evaluation of internal controls, processes, and risk management frameworks for operational excellence.

Trust & Co-operative Society Audit

Specialized audit services for charitable trusts and co-operative societies per applicable statutes.

GST Registration

Smooth and compliant GST registration for businesses, e-commerce operators, and interstate suppliers.

Monthly / Quarterly Returns

Accurate and timely filing of GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and GSTR-7 to ensure zero penalties.

GST Annual Return

Comprehensive GSTR-9 and GSTR-9C filing with thorough reconciliation and audit trail.

GST Reconciliation

Detailed reconciliation of GSTR-2A/2B with books of accounts to maximise ITC claims.

GST Refund Claim

Expert handling of GST refund applications for exporters, inverted duty, and excess tax paid.

GST Litigation Services

Representation before GST authorities, appellate tribunals, and High Courts for disputes and notices.

GST Cancellation

Streamlined cancellation of GST registration for businesses that have ceased or restructured.

ITR Filing

Accurate filing of all ITR forms for individuals, HUFs, partnerships, companies, and LLPs.

TDS / TCS Compliance

End-to-end TDS return filing, form 16/16A generation, and TCS compliance management.

Advance Tax Calculation

Strategic advance tax planning and computation to avoid interest liability under Sections 234B & 234C.

Tax Planning & Advisory

Year-round tax planning strategies to legally minimise tax outgo for individuals and businesses.

Representation before IT Authorities

Professional representation in scrutiny assessments, appeals, and tax hearings.

Income Tax Litigation

Expert legal support for CIT(A), ITAT proceedings, and High Court appeals in tax disputes.

TAN & PAN Application

Quick and error-free TAN and PAN registration for individuals, businesses, and trusts.

Company Incorporation

Seamless incorporation of Private Limited, Public Limited, and One-Person Companies under MCA.

LLP Incorporation

Fast and compliant registration of Limited Liability Partnerships with complete documentation support.

ROC Annual Filing

Timely filing of AOC-4, MGT-7, and other ROC forms to maintain company compliance status.

Charge Related Compliance

Creation, modification, and satisfaction of charges over company assets as per Companies Act 2013.

Other MCA Compliances

Director KYC, DIN allotment, board resolutions, share transfers, and all other MCA filings.

Udyam / MSME Registration

Online Udyam registration to unlock government schemes, collateral-free loans, and priority sector benefits.

Food Licence (FSSAI)

FSSAI basic, state, and central licence registration and renewal for food businesses.

Trademark Registration

Protect your brand identity with trademark search, filing, and prosecution services.

PF / ESIC Registration

Hassle-free PF and ESIC employer registration for compliant workforce management.

Business Consultancy

Strategic financial and operational guidance to scale your business sustainably and profitably.

Accounting & Book Keeping

Accurate, cloud-ready bookkeeping and MIS reporting for well-informed business decisions.

CMA Data & Loan Project Report

Bank-grade CMA data preparation and project reports to support working capital and term loan applications.

PF / ESIC Return

Monthly EPF and ESIC return filing with challan generation and employee reconciliation.

Start-up Advisory

End-to-end start-up handholding: incorporation, DPIIT recognition, funding advisory, and compliance.

Certification Services

All types of CA certificates including net worth, turnover, export, IT refund, and bank NOC certificates.

Digital Signature Certificate

Class 2 and Class 3 DSC issuance for individuals and organizations for MCA, GST, and tender filings.
